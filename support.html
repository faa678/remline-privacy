常见问题

闹钟到点不响？

Remline 的闹钟由系统闹钟服务托管，在锁屏、静音、专注模式下都会响铃。如果不响，请到系统设置 ▸ Remline 确认已允许「闹钟」权限；并确保闹钟处于开启状态、时间设置正确。

闹钟只震动、没有声音？

请打开 App 内 铃声设置，检查「闹钟铃声」总开关是否被关闭（关闭后所有闹钟会静音、仅震动）。你也可以为每个闹钟单独设置铃声，或将其设为「仅震动」。

想调整震动 / 触觉反馈？

进入 我的 ▸ 触觉反馈，可开关触觉，并选择反馈强度（轻 / 中 / 强），设置会应用到全 App 的操作震动。

贪睡（稍后提醒）怎么用？

闹钟响起时，可在锁屏的实时活动卡片 / 灵动岛上直接点「贪睡」或「完成」。

免费版能建几个闹钟？

免费版最多可创建 20 个闹钟。升级 Remline Pro 后不限数量，并解锁多事件闹钟、iCloud 同步与作息打卡记录。

如何恢复已购买的 Pro？

换设备或重装后，打开 App 内的 Pro 升级页，点「恢复购买」即可恢复（需登录当初购买所用的 Apple ID）。

如何管理或取消订阅？

前往 系统设置 ▸ 点按你的 Apple ID ▸ 订阅，找到 Remline 即可管理或取消。订阅会在当前周期结束时自动续订，除非在结束前至少 24 小时取消。永久买断为一次性付费，无需续订。

iCloud 同步不生效？

iCloud 同步为 Pro 权益，需在 我的 ▸ 同步设置 中开启，且两台设备需登录同一 Apple ID 并已开启 iCloud。数据存放在你本人的 iCloud 账户中，开发者无法访问。

数据安全吗？

Remline 无账号、无服务器，你的闹钟与打卡仅保存在设备本机（开启 iCloud 同步时存于你本人的 iCloud）。详见 隐私政策。

Frequently Asked Questions

My alarm doesn't ring

Remline's alarms are powered by the system alarm service and ring even when your phone is locked, silenced, or in Focus mode. If an alarm doesn't ring, go to Settings ▸ Remline and make sure the alarm permission is allowed, and that the alarm is enabled and set to the correct time.

It only vibrates, with no sound

Open Sound Settings in the app and check whether the master "Alarm Sound" toggle is off (when off, all alarms are silent and vibrate only). You can also set a ringtone per alarm, or set an alarm to vibrate only.

Adjusting vibration / haptics

Go to Me ▸ Haptics to toggle haptics and choose a strength (Light / Medium / Strong); it applies to haptic feedback across the app.

How does snooze work?

When an alarm fires, tap Snooze or Complete directly on the Live Activity card / Dynamic Island on the lock screen.

How many alarms can the free plan have?

The free plan allows up to 20 alarms. Remline Pro removes the limit and unlocks multi-step alarms, iCloud sync, and check-in history.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

After switching or reinstalling, open the Pro upgrade page in the app and tap "Restore Purchases" (sign in with the Apple ID used for the original purchase).

How do I manage or cancel a subscription?

Go to Settings ▸ tap your Apple ID ▸ Subscriptions and select Remline to manage or cancel. Subscriptions auto-renew at the end of each period unless cancelled at least 24 hours before it ends. The lifetime purchase is one-time and does not renew.

iCloud sync isn't working

iCloud sync is a Pro feature. Enable it under Me ▸ Sync Settings; both devices must be signed into the same Apple ID with iCloud enabled. Data lives in your own iCloud account and is not accessible to the developer.

Is my data safe?

Remline has no accounts and no server. Your alarms and check-ins are stored only on your device (and, if iCloud sync is on, in your own iCloud). See the Privacy Policy.
