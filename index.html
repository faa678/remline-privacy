Remline 隐私政策

最后更新：2026-06-16

Remline 是一款完全在你设备本机运行的闹钟 / 作息提醒 App。我们的核心原则是：不收集、不上传、不追踪你的任何数据。

我们收集的数据

无。Remline 没有账号系统、没有服务器后端。你创建的闹钟、提醒、打卡记录、昵称、头像以及全部设置，都仅保存在你的设备本机，不会发送给我们或任何第三方。

iCloud 同步（可选）

Pro 会员可主动开启 iCloud 同步。开启后，你的闹钟、打卡、昵称与头像会通过 Apple 的 iCloud 在你自己登录同一 Apple ID 的设备之间同步。这些数据存放在你本人的 iCloud 账户中，由 Apple 管理并受 Apple 隐私政策约束；我们（开发者）无法访问这些数据。该功能默认关闭，你可随时关闭。

位置

若你授权位置权限，Remline 仅用它在首页显示你当前所在城市的名称。定位结果在设备本机处理，不会被存储或上传。你可以随时在系统设置中关闭位置权限，不影响闹钟功能。

照片

设置头像时，你通过系统相册选择器挑选一张图片。所选图片仅保存在设备本机用于显示头像，不会上传。Remline 不读取你的相册其余内容。

购买与订阅

Remline Pro 提供月度订阅、年度订阅（自动续订）与永久买断三种方式，均通过 Apple App Store 内购完成。订阅会在每个计费周期结束时自动续订，除非你在当前周期结束前至少 24 小时取消；可随时在 设置 ▸ Apple ID ▸ 订阅 中管理或取消。所有交易由 Apple 处理，受 Apple 隐私政策约束；Remline 不接触、不存储你的支付信息。

追踪与第三方

Remline 不含任何第三方 SDK、广告、分析或追踪组件，不进行跨 App 追踪。

儿童

Remline 不收集任何个人数据，适合所有年龄使用。

政策变更

若本政策有更新，我们会修改本页面的「最后更新」日期。

Remline Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-16

Remline is an alarm / daily-routine reminder app that runs entirely on your device. Our core principle: we do not collect, transmit, or track any of your data.

Data We Collect

None. Remline has no accounts and no server backend. Your alarms, reminders, completion history, nickname, avatar, and all settings are stored only on your device and are never sent to us or any third party.

iCloud Sync (optional)

Pro members can opt in to iCloud sync. When enabled, your alarms, completion data, nickname and avatar sync via Apple's iCloud between your own devices signed into the same Apple ID. This data resides in your own iCloud account, managed by Apple under the Apple Privacy Policy; we (the developer) cannot access it. This feature is off by default and can be turned off anytime.

Location

If you grant location permission, Remline uses it solely to display your current city name on the home screen. It is processed on-device and is never stored or uploaded. You may revoke location access anytime in Settings without affecting alarms.

Photos

When setting an avatar, you pick an image via the system photo picker. The chosen image is stored locally on your device for display only and is never uploaded. Remline does not read the rest of your photo library.

Purchases & Subscriptions

Remline Pro is offered as a monthly subscription, an annual subscription (auto-renewing), or a one-time lifetime purchase, all through Apple App Store in-app purchase. Subscriptions auto-renew at the end of each billing period unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the period ends; you can manage or cancel anytime in Settings ▸ Apple ID ▸ Subscriptions. All transactions are handled by Apple under the Apple Privacy Policy; Remline never sees or stores your payment information.

Tracking & Third Parties

Remline contains no third-party SDKs, ads, analytics, or tracking, and performs no cross-app tracking.

Children

Remline collects no personal data and is suitable for all ages.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date on this page.
